Game Fishing - Deep Sea

Experience the Thrill of the Open Ocean!

Embark on an exhilarating journey into Zanzibar’s deep blue waters, where the sea is teeming with prized fish and breathtaking scenery. This is an unforgettable adventure combining the excitement of fishing with the beauty of the ocean, creating unique memories.

What to Expect:

  • Professional Guidance: Whether you're an experienced angler or a beginner, our expert team will assist you every step of the way.
  • Prime Fishing Spots: Venture to rich fishing grounds filled with species like marlin, tuna, barracuda, sailfish, and mahi-mahi.
  • Top-Notch Gear: Enjoy the best fishing experience with high-quality rods, reels, and bait provided.
  • Modern Boat: Our boat is fully equipped with safety and comfort features, including shaded areas for relaxation.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Half-day or full-day options available.
  • Start Time: Flexible to fit your schedule.
  • Cost: $250 for a private boat (no sharing).

Perfect For:

  • Fishing enthusiasts looking for a big catch.
  • Families, friends, or couples seeking a unique and thrilling day out.
  • Anyone craving the excitement of fishing while enjoying Zanzibar’s stunning ocean views.
