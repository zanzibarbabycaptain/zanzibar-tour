Dolphin Tour

Swim with Zanzibar’s Playful Dolphins!

Experience the thrill of swimming alongside dolphins in the crystal-clear waters of Zanzibar. This tour provides a unique opportunity to connect with these intelligent and friendly marine creatures in their natural habitat.

What to Expect:

  • Close Dolphin Encounters: Witness these majestic animals up close and observe their playful behavior.
  • Snorkeling Opportunity: Dive into the warm waters for a closer view of the vibrant marine life.
  • Scenic Boat Ride: Enjoy a picturesque journey to the dolphin hotspots, guided by experienced captains.
  • Professional Guidance: Learn about dolphins and their ecosystem from knowledgeable guides.

Why Choose the Dolphin Tour?

This tour is perfect for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts looking for an unforgettable experience. Swimming with dolphins in their natural habitat is a truly magical and memorable activity.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 2-3 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, early morning for the best chance to see dolphins.
  • Cost: Price on call.

Perfect For:

  • Adventure lovers eager to swim with dolphins.
  • Families and friends looking for a fun and unique outing.
  • Nature enthusiasts seeking to explore Zanzibar’s marine life.
