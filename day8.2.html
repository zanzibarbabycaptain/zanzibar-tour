Spice Tour

Experience Zanzibar’s World of Spices!

Embark on a captivating journey through Zanzibar’s famous spice farms. Discover the rich history, vibrant culture, and exotic flavors that have earned Zanzibar the nickname "The Spice Island."

What to Expect:

  • Guided Farm Tour: Walk through lush plantations and learn about the cultivation and uses of various spices, herbs, and fruits.
  • Aromatic Discoveries: Smell, taste, and identify fresh spices like cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and vanilla.
  • Cultural Insights: Gain a deeper understanding of how spices influence Zanzibar’s cuisine, medicine, and traditions.
  • Interactive Experience: Participate in activities like peeling fresh coconuts or grinding spices.
  • Local Shopping: Purchase fresh spices, handmade soaps, and local products as souvenirs.

Why Choose the Spice Tour?

The Spice Tour is a must-do experience for anyone visiting Zanzibar. It’s an educational, sensory, and interactive adventure that connects you to the island’s heritage and showcases its natural beauty.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 2-5 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, morning and afternoon sessions available.
  • Cost: $40 per person (private tour).

Perfect For:

  • Food enthusiasts and home cooks interested in fresh spices.
  • Families and friends looking for an interactive and educational outing.
  • Travelers eager to explore Zanzibar’s cultural heritage.
