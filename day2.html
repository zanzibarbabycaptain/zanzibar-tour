ABOUT AQUARIUM TORTOISE TOUR

Welcome to Baraka Natural Aquarium Come to Zanzibar’s Best Turtles Aquarium and enjoy the chance to see, feed and swim with these beautiful native animals. Our turtles are brought to us after they have been caught in fisherman’s net and have been injured and once they reach a certain size we release them back into the Ocean This trip offers unique opportunities to learn more about how traditional dhow boats are built and all about the natural preservation of turtles. See beautiful beaches and get a chance to swim with turtles. Included Professional Tour Guide Entrance Fees Pickup and drop-off transport Government Tax Fees

Highlights of the Tour:

  • Close interaction with marine turtles.
  • Learn about conservation and protection programs.
  • Great for families, kids, and animal lovers.
