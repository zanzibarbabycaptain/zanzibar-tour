Local Fishing Tour

Experience Traditional Fishing in Zanzibar!

Join us for a unique local fishing experience, where you’ll learn traditional fishing techniques and enjoy the serene beauty of Zanzibar’s coastal waters. This is a perfect way to connect with the local culture and explore the ocean in a traditional fishing dhow.

What to Expect:

  • Traditional Techniques: Learn and practice the art of fishing using age-old methods passed down through generations.
  • Local Dhow Experience: Sail in an authentic wooden fishing dhow crafted by local artisans.
  • Scenic Ocean Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of Zanzibar’s turquoise waters as you fish.
  • Guided Adventure: Our friendly and knowledgeable guides will ensure you have an enjoyable and safe experience.

Why Choose the Local Fishing Tour?

This tour offers an authentic and interactive way to experience Zanzibar’s fishing heritage. Whether you’re an avid fisherman or a curious traveler, you’ll enjoy the peaceful waters and hands-on learning.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 3-4 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, morning or afternoon sessions.
  • Cost: $150 for a private tour (no sharing).

Perfect For:

  • Travelers seeking an authentic cultural experience.
  • Fishing enthusiasts of all skill levels.
  • Families and friends looking for a fun and educational outing.
