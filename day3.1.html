Sunset Cruise

Experience the Magic of Zanzibar’s Sunset!

Embark on a peaceful and romantic evening cruise along Zanzibar’s coastlines as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean. Watch the sky change into a breathtaking canvas of warm hues while the gentle waves serenade your journey.

What to Expect:

  • Relaxing Atmosphere: Enjoy the tranquil environment as you sail across the calm waters of the Indian Ocean.
  • Stunning Sunset Views: Witness one of nature’s most beautiful spectacles as the sun dips into the horizon.
  • Comfortable Dhow: Sail on a traditional wooden dhow equipped for comfort and style.
  • Optional Refreshments: Indulge in a selection of beverages (on request) as you enjoy the scenery.

Why Choose a Sunset Cruise in Zanzibar?

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Zanzibar’s coastlines while enjoying the serenity of the evening. The Sunset Cruise is perfect for creating unforgettable moments with loved ones or simply unwinding after a long day of exploration.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: 4 hours (evening).
  • Start Time: Between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM (depending on the season).
  • Cost: $50/person for a private cruise, $20/person for a shared experience.

Perfect For:

  • Couples looking for a romantic getaway.
  • Families and friends seeking a peaceful and picturesque experience.
  • Nature lovers and photographers wanting to capture stunning sunset views.
