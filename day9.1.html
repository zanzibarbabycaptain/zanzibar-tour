Prison Island Tour

Step into History and Nature at Prison Island!

Discover the fascinating history and natural beauty of Prison Island, also known as Changuu Island. From giant tortoises to pristine beaches, this tour offers a blend of education, relaxation, and adventure.

What to Expect:

  • Giant Tortoises: Meet and interact with the island’s famous Aldabra giant tortoises, some of which are over 100 years old.
  • Historical Sites: Explore the old prison buildings and learn about the island’s intriguing past.
  • Snorkeling: Dive into the crystal-clear waters and marvel at vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Scenic Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the turquoise waters and surrounding islands.

Why Choose the Prison Island Tour?

This tour combines history, wildlife, and natural beauty, making it a perfect choice for families, history enthusiasts, and nature lovers. It’s an unforgettable experience just a short boat ride from Stone Town.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 3-5 hours.
  • Availability: Daily.
  • Cost: Price on call.

Perfect For:

  • Families and friends looking for a unique outing.
  • History buffs interested in Zanzibar’s past.
  • Nature enthusiasts eager to see giant tortoises and marine life.
