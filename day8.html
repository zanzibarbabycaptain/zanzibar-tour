Half Day Trip - Mnemba Island

Discover the Beauty of Mnemba Island!

Join us for an incredible half-day adventure to Mnemba Island that combine swimming with dolphins,white Sandbank,starfish,snorkeling. Perfect for those with limited time, this trip offers a chance to experience the best of Zanzibar’s marine life, pristine beaches, and vibrant coral reefs.

What to Expect:

  • Snorkeling at Mnemba: Explore colorful coral reefs and encounter a variety of marine life in the crystal-clear waters.
  • Dolphin Spotting: Start your trip with the opportunity to swim alongside dolphins in their natural habitat.
  • White Sandbank: Relax and soak up the sun on a beautiful sandbank surrounded by turquoise waters.

Why Choose the Half Day Trip to Mnemba?

This trip is ideal for travelers who want to enjoy the highlights of Mnemba Island without committing to a full-day excursion. It’s perfect for snorkeling enthusiasts, nature lovers, and anyone looking to unwind in paradise.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Half Day (8:30 AM - 12:30 PM).
  • Availability: Daily.
  • Cost: $150 for a private tour (&50/person for sharing tour).

Perfect For:

  • Travelers with limited time looking for a quick yet memorable adventure.
  • Snorkeling enthusiasts eager to explore Zanzibar’s marine life.
  • Families and friends seeking a fun and relaxing outing.
