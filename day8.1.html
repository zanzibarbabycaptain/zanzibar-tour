Nungwi Village Tour

Discover the Heart of Nungwi!

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and traditions of Zanzibar with a guided tour of Nungwi Village. Explore the daily life of the locals, visit traditional craftsmen, and learn about the vibrant history of this iconic fishing village.

What to Expect:

  • Local Culture: Get an authentic insight into the lives of the people of Nungwi.
  • Dhow Building: Witness skilled craftsmen constructing traditional wooden dhows.
  • Seaweed Farms: Learn about the importance of seaweed farming to the local economy.
  • Historical Sites: Visit landmarks that showcase the rich history and heritage of the village.
  • Local Markets: Stroll through vibrant markets filled with spices, fresh produce, and handmade crafts.

Why Choose the Nungwi Village Tour?

This tour offers a unique opportunity to connect with the people of Zanzibar, learn about their traditions, and experience the island’s cultural heritage firsthand. Perfect for travelers seeking meaningful interactions and authentic experiences.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 3-4 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, morning and afternoon sessions available.
  • Cost: Price on call.

Perfect For:

  • Travelers interested in Zanzibar’s culture and history.
  • Families and friends looking for an educational and interactive experience.
  • Anyone eager to support local communities and artisans.
