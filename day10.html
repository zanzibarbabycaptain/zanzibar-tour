Stone Town Tour

Explore the Historic Heart of Zanzibar!

Step back in time and wander through the narrow streets of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This tour offers a glimpse into Zanzibar’s rich history, diverse culture, and architectural wonders.

What to Expect:

  • Historical Sites: Visit iconic landmarks such as the Old Fort, House of Wonders, and the Sultan’s Palace.
  • Slave Market: Learn about Zanzibar’s role in the slave trade with a visit to the former slave market and Anglican Cathedral.
  • Local Markets: Stroll through bustling markets filled with spices, crafts, and fresh produce.
  • Cultural Insights: Discover Zanzibar’s diverse heritage influenced by Arab, Persian, Indian, and European cultures.
  • Freddie Mercury’s House: See the birthplace of the legendary Queen frontman, Freddie Mercury.

Why Choose the Stone Town Tour?

This tour is perfect for history buffs, culture enthusiasts, and anyone looking to experience the authentic charm of Zanzibar. Stone Town is a living museum, offering a unique blend of the past and present.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 3-4 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, morning and afternoon sessions available.
  • Cost: Price on call.

Perfect For:

  • History lovers eager to learn about Zanzibar’s past.
  • Travelers interested in diverse cultures and traditions.
  • Families and friends seeking an educational and interactive experience.
