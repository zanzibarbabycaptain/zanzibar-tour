Tumbatu Island Snorkeling

Discover the Hidden Beauty of Tumbatu Island!

Escape to the serene waters of Tumbatu Island, a hidden gem of Zanzibar. This snorkeling tour offers a chance to explore vibrant coral reefs, colorful marine life, and the unspoiled beauty of the island’s underwater world.

What to Expect:

  • Snorkeling Adventure: Explore stunning coral reefs and encounter an array of marine species.
  • Pristine Waters: Swim in the crystal-clear waters surrounding the island.
  • Scenic Boat Ride: Enjoy a relaxing journey to and from Tumbatu Island aboard a traditional dhow.
  • Expert Guidance: Our professional guides will ensure you have a safe and enjoyable snorkeling experience.

Why Choose Tumbatu Island Snorkeling?

Tumbatu Island is known for its untouched reefs and tranquil atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for snorkeling enthusiasts. Whether you're an experienced snorkeler or a first-timer, this tour provides a memorable way to explore Zanzibar’s underwater wonders.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 4 hours.
  • Availability: Daily, subject to weather conditions.
  • Cost: $50/person.and dependeds on number of people. for a private tour (no sharing). Descount is available

Perfect For:

  • Adventure seekers who love snorkeling.
  • Families and friends looking for a serene ocean escape.
  • Nature lovers eager to discover Zanzibar’s marine biodiversity.
