Taxi Boat - Nungwi to Kendwa

Fast, Reliable, and Scenic Transfers!

Travel between Nungwi and Kendwa in style with our convenient taxi boat service. Enjoy the breathtaking coastal views and the gentle sea breeze as you quickly and safely reach your destination.

What to Expect:

  • Quick Transfers: Reach your destination in just a few minutes, avoiding long road trips.
  • Scenic Views: Enjoy the stunning coastline of Zanzibar during your ride.
  • Professional Crew: Our experienced crew ensures a smooth and safe journey.
  • Flexibility: Choose your preferred departure time for maximum convenience.

Why Choose Our Taxi Boat?

Our taxi boat service is the perfect way to travel between Nungwi and Kendwa. Skip the traffic and experience the beauty of the ocean while saving time. Whether you're heading to a resort, a beach, or an adventure, our service ensures you arrive relaxed and ready to enjoy your day.

Service Details:

  • Duration: Approx. 10-15 minutes.
  • Availability: Daily transfers available from morning to evening.
  • Cost: $10-$50, depending on the number of passengers and time of travel.

Perfect For:

  • Travelers looking for a quick and scenic transfer.
  • Families, couples, or groups heading to resorts or beaches.
  • Anyone wanting to enjoy a short yet memorable boat ride.
